Lady Advisory & Internal Complaints Committee

Objective:To empower women in all spheres of life, for the betterment of self, family and society.

  • To help women understand their strength and potential.
  • To create awareness among women on occupational, legal and constitutional rights.
    • To educate lady faculty and girl students on women specific health issues and overall well being.
  • To promote a culture of respect and equality for female gender.
  • To combat and eradicate the menace of sexual harassment.

*Note: For a deeper understanding of sexual harassment of women at workplace, please check the handbook issued

Form of Action:

  • To organize guest lectures on areas covering legal, career and social aspects by inviting successful women personnel from varied sectors like education, health, self-defence, yoga and meditation.
  • To conduct rallies and public awareness campaigns on women education and women related issues.
  • To mount display boards, quotes and writings in the favour of respect for women in the vicinity of the campus.
  • To encourage discussion forums to voice out the atrocities against women.
  • To register and address the complaints and problems of girl students and lady staff.
